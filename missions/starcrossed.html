Starcrossed

Year/Season: 2023 (Season of the Wish)

Location: Overland, Black Garden

Exotic Reward: Wish-Keeper

Mission Overview

Starcrossed is a mission in the Black Garden where Guardians must defeat Akardon, Pitiless Mind and overcome challenging encounters to earn the Wish-Keeper exotic bow.

Key Bosses/Encounters
