A Hollow Coronation

Year/Season: 2021 (Season of the Lost)

Location: Dozmary Vault, Dreaming City

Exotic Reward: Ager's Scepter

Mission Overview

A Hollow Coronation is the final step in the Ager's Scepter questline. Guardians must enter the Dozmary Vault in the Dreaming City, solve puzzles, and defeat enemies to claim the exotic trace rifle Ager's Scepter.

Key Bosses/Encounters

  • Kelgorath, Risen from Bones
