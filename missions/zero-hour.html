Zero Hour

Year/Season: 2019 (Season of the Drifter)

Location: The Old Tower, The Last City

Exotic Reward: Outbreak Perfected

Mission Overview

Zero Hour is a secret timed mission set in the ruins of the old Tower. Players must navigate complex platforming sections, solve puzzles, and defeat Fallen enemies to reach the final boss within a time limit. The mission is known for its high difficulty and unique setting.

Key Bosses/Encounters

  • Siriks, Loyal to Eramis
  • Mimiks-0, Devil's Scion
  • Mimiks-1, Devil's Scion
