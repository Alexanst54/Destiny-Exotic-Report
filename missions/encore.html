Encore

Year/Season: 2024 (Episode: Echoes)

Location: Arcadian Valley, Nessus

Exotic Reward: Choir of One

Mission Overview

Encore is a mission on Nessus where Guardians must defeat Parodos, Stasimon, and Exodos, Choral Minds, to earn the Choir of One exotic weapon. The mission features Vex enemies and musical puzzle elements.

Key Bosses/Encounters

  • Parodos, Choral Mind (Overture)
  • Stasimon, Choral Mind (Concerto)
  • Exodos, Choral Mind (Coda)
