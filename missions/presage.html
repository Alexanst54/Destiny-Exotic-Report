Presage

Year/Season: 2021 (Season of the Chosen)

Location: The Glykon, Reef

Exotic Reward: Dead Man's Tale

Mission Overview

Presage is a horror-themed mission set aboard the derelict Cabal ship Glykon. Guardians must navigate haunted corridors, solve environmental puzzles, and face Scorn enemies to uncover the fate of the crew and claim Dead Man's Tale.
