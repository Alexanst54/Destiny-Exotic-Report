Dual Destiny

Year/Season: 2024 (The Final Shape)

Location: The Refraction, The Pale Heart

Exotic Reward: Relativism (Hunter), Solipsism (Warlock), Stoicism (Titan)

Mission Overview

Dual Destiny is a unique two-player mission in The Pale Heart. Guardians must work together to solve puzzles and defeat Expector Selin and Expector Yemiq to earn class-specific exotic class items.

Key Bosses/Encounters

  • Expector Selin
  • Expector Yemiq
