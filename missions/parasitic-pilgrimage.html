Parasitic Pilgrimage

Year/Season: 2022 (The Witch Queen)

Location: Savathûn's Throne World

Exotic Reward: Parasite

Mission Overview

Parasitic Pilgrimage is the final step in the Parasite questline. Guardians must traverse Savathûn's Throne World, defeat Hive and Scorn enemies, and complete objectives to earn the Parasite exotic grenade launcher.

Key Bosses/Encounters
