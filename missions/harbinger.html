Harbinger

Year/Season: 2021 (Season of the Hunt)

Location: Trostland, EDZ

Exotic Reward: Hawkmoon

Mission Overview

Harbinger is a secret mission in the European Dead Zone, where players must investigate mysterious signals, solve platforming puzzles, and defeat powerful Taken and Scorn enemies. The mission is required to earn random rolls and the catalyst for Hawkmoon.

Key Bosses/Encounters

  • Akorith, Lightdrinker of Savathûn
  • Ishrasek, Lightbinder of Savathûn
