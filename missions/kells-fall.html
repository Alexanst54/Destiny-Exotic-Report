Kell’s Fall

Year/Season: 2024 (Episode: Revenant)

Location: Revenant Fortress, Reef

Exotic Reward: Slayer's Fang

Mission Overview

Kell’s Fall is a mission in the Reef where Guardians must defeat Reksis Vahn, Screeb Matriarch, and Fikrul, the Fanatic to earn the Slayer's Fang exotic weapon. The mission features Scorn enemies and multi-phase boss fights.

Key Bosses/Encounters

  • Reksis Vahn, the Hangman (Reflection)
  • Screeb Matriarch (Distortion)
  • Fikrul, the Fanatic (Diffraction)
