The Other Side

Year/Season: 2019 (Season of Opulence)

Location: Ascendant Leviathan, Leviathan

Exotic Reward: Bad Juju

Mission Overview

The Other Side is the final step of the Bad Juju questline. Guardians enter the Ascendant Realm aboard the Leviathan to defeat Taken enemies and claim the Bad Juju pulse rifle. The mission features unique platforming and combat challenges.

Key Bosses/Encounters

  • Graam, Born of the Hellmouth
  • Might of Riven
  • Mant Yût, Born of the Hellmouth
