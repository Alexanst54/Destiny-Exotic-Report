What Remains

Year/Season: 2023 (Lightfall)

Location: Boundless Horizon, Black Garden

Exotic Reward: Deterministic Chaos

Mission Overview

What Remains is a mission in the Black Garden where Guardians face the Conceptual Mind to earn the Deterministic Chaos exotic machine gun. The mission features Vex enemies and unique environmental challenges.

Key Bosses/Encounters
