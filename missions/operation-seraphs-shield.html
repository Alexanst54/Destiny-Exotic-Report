Operation: Seraph’s Shield

Year/Season: 2022 (Season of the Seraph)

Location: Seraph Station, Earth

Exotic Reward: Revision Zero

Mission Overview

Operation: Seraph’s Shield is a mission set aboard a space station in Earth's orbit. Guardians must bypass security systems, solve puzzles, and defeat Praksis the Defiled to earn the Revision Zero exotic pulse rifle.

Key Bosses/Encounters

  • Praksis the Defiled
